How To Care for Huskies?

Feeding

Provide a balanced diet with high-quality with high-quality dog food formulated for active breeds like huskies. Feed them twice a day, following the recommended portion sizes based on their age, weight, and activity level. Always keep fresh water available.

Grooming

Brush their thick double coat regularly to prevent matting. Use a slicker brush or an undercoat rake to remove lose hair. Bathe them only when necessary using dog-specific shampoo. Trim their nails carefully to avoid overgrowth.

Exercise and Mental Stimulation

Huskies need at least 1-2 hours of exercise per day. Take them for brisk walks, jogs, or playtime in a secure area. Keep their minds sharp with puzzle toys, interactive games, and/or training sessions.

Healthcare

Schedule regular check-ups for vaccinations, parasite prevention, and dental care. Keep them cool in hot weather and provide shade and fresh water.
